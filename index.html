Vehicle Health

Keep your car healthy between visits

Small habits that prevent big repair bills — especially in Gulf heat and dust.

Check tire pressure monthly

Under-inflated tires wear faster, hurt fuel economy, and raise blowout risk in summer heat.

Stick to your oil schedule

Fresh oil protects the engine. Skipping changes is the single biggest cause of early engine wear.

Never ignore a warning light

A light on the dashboard is your car asking for help early — before it becomes an expensive problem.

Keep battery terminals clean

Corrosion build-up is a common cause of no-start issues, especially with heavy AC use in summer.

Listen for new noises

Grinding, squealing, or knocking sounds rarely fix themselves. Catching them early saves money.

Service AC before peak summer

Have the system checked before the heat hits hard — refrigerant and compressor issues get worse fast.
